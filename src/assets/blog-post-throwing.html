There is a few different techniques when it comes to make a cocktail, you can mix and stir the ingredients in a mixing glass, you can use various shakers, you can build them in the glass where you planning to serve or you can throw a cocktail too!

Throwing techniques is one of the oldest one and it roots back to Spain, in the 17th century a reference is found to the throwing of alcohol and to this day in the Basque region Spanish cider throwers drop cider from a height. Later on this technique have been carried to Cuba, and then back to Spain, by a bartender named Miguel Boadas who had trained at Floridita. He opened a cocktail bar in Barcelona in 1933 named Boadas and this became the only place in the world for nearly 50 years where drinks were thrown.

In these days there is a lot of great bars around the globe where you can find thrown cocktails by professional bartenders as this elegant and classy style of making drinks living its renaissance.
