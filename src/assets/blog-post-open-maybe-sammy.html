Maybe Sammy OPEN

After a lot of planning, preparation and hard work we are happy to announce our new project Maybe Sammy opened its doors in the first week of January. Maybe Sammy is inspired by the Rat Pack and their memorable entertaining style, our main focus is hospitality through well-crafted classic cocktails and experimental signature creations.

Every member of our team has a serious background from 5 star hotels from around the world, such as the Savoy and the Langham in London or the Shangri-la hotel in Hong Kong. We also have a genuine coffee program and a delicious snack menu with signature bagels, charcuterie and cheese plates.

We are looking forward to welcome you in Sydney, Australia in the Rocks, on Harrington street.

Balazs Molnar Januar 2019 Opening, Maybe Sammy
