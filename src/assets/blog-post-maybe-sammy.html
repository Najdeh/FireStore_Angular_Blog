Maybe Sammy x Origin Bar guest shift

It’s always a pleasure to be invited for a guest shift in a different country and it’s even bigger pleasure if you have the chance to work with a friend together again after many-many years. In November we headed to Singapore, to Origin Bar which is located in the prestigious Shangri-La Hotel to serve for the first time ever our signature cocktails from our new project, Maybe Sammy. Maybe Sammy is a neo classic cocktail bar located in the city center of Sydney which is opening in January, 2019 but as a sneak peek we brought 5 of our creations from our upcoming first menu.

We served many different style of cocktails starting from a pre bottled “Maybe-Mini” sipping gin drink with a touch of mint, through the long refreshing rum based Bonanza with fruity flavors to the “Circus Circus” which been served with a magic trick!

Maybe Sammy is representing the glamourous style of a hotel bar from the roaring 60’s inspired by the unbelievable entertainment of the Rat Pack. Our first menu contains signature cocktails, mini-cocktails and exclusive “high roller” cocktails for the big players. The drinks are very complex in flavors and clean and classy in look, and all of them named after famous Casinos and card rooms of the era of the Rat Pack.
